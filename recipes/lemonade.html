Fresh Lemonade

Description

A summer classic, this lemonade is easy and refreshing too!

Ingredients

  • 10 lemons
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 4 cups crushed ice

Steps

  1. Cut 1/2 of one lemon into thin slices and set aside.
  2. Juice remaining 9 1/2 lemons and pour into a glass pitcher or punch bowl.
  3. Stir in the water and sugar until dissolved.
  4. Pour in the crushed ice and float the lemon slices on top.
